Attractions

Attractions

Discover a diverse array of attractions at our park, each designed to ignite your sense of adventure and wonder.

  • Break Dance

    BreakDance attraction whisks thrill-seekers into a pulsating whirlwind of spins, tilts, and gravity-defying maneuvers, creating an electrifying dance of adrenaline and excitement.

  • Disco Dancer

    DiscoDancer at the amusement park transforms the dance floor into a kaleidoscopic spectacle, combining dazzling lights, infectious beats, and exhilarating spins for a groovy and energetic experience.

  • Twister

    Twister at the amusement park twists and turns riders through a thrilling vortex of high-speed rotations, delivering an exhilarating and dizzying adventure for those seeking a whirlwind of excitement.

  • Extreme

    "Extreme, the heart-pounding attraction at the amusement park, catapults riders into an adrenaline-fueled adventure with its daring loops, sharp twists, and breathtaking drops, ensuring an intense and unforgettable experience for thrill-seekers.

  • Bumping Cars

    Crashing Cars at the amusement park invites participants into a chaotic collision course, where the joyous chaos of bumping and crashing combines with the thrill of competition, creating a spirited and entertaining ride for all ages.

  • Ferris Wheel

    Imagine a grand Ferris wheel standing tall at the heart of Lunpark Valentino, its intricate metal framework illuminated by a soft cascade of lights. As it slowly turns, each gondola gently lifts riders above the park, offering breathtaking views of the vibrant surroundings.

About Us

Where Generations Unite & in Joyful Delight

Welcome to LunaPark Valentino, a cherished gem in the heart of Bulgaria's entertainment landscape. Our journey began three generations ago when our visionary and illustrious grandfather set the stage for what would become one of the most beloved LunaParks in the entire country. In the early days, under the guidance of our pioneering grandfather, LunaPark Valentino emerged as a testament to his innovative spirit and unwavering commitment to providing unparalleled joy to the community. His legacy is woven into the very fabric of LunaPark Valentino, as he brought forth new ideas that set the foundation for the park's success.
